Password strength


Securing your account in any system is important. By maintaining strong unique passwords, you keep your accounts and the systems you use, secure.

To put it simply, the longer the password, the better. Adding extra numbers and special characters can help.

It's a lot easier than you think. Instead of using a common word plus some numbers, try a combination of random words and spaces. Example:

Soccer
Book
Dog
Whale

4 random words (with spaces) can easily make a long password that's not too hard to remember.
